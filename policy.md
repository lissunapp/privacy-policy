## 3. Health data — access, collection, and use

The App processes data in a **therapy / health-related** context. We access and collect:

- **Microphone audio** of therapy sessions when you **start recording** and the booking allows recording.
- **Session notes**, **marked moments/observations**, and **goal/progress** data linked to sessions.
- **Scheduling/session identifiers** associated with specific clients/services.

**Uses:** record and **upload** audio to **secure cloud storage** (via our API); sync notes/observations/goals with our **backend**; run session lifecycle (start/end, prep, tasks).

We **do not sell** this data. We **do not use** session audio or clinical notes for **advertising** or unrelated **marketing**.

| Data | Retention |

## 8. Deletion — how to remove your data

To request **deletion** or **correction** of your personal data:

1. Email **[reach-us@lissun.app](mailto:reach-us@lissun.app)** with your registered **phone or email**, **name**, and a clear request (e.g. account deletion, or export then deletion).

We will **verify your identity** and respond within **30 days** (or as required by applicable law). Where **law requires retention** of professional or health records, we will explain any limitation and delete data when the retention period ends. Backups may persist for a short technical window before purge.

You may also use **in-app support** to request deletion where available.

The App is **not** directed at children as end-users; therapists use it professionally.
